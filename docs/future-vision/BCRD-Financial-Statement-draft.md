## 1. Statutory Capital Reserve

Per Article 15 of the Reglamento de Sistemas de Pago:

- Initial deposit: RD$15,000,000 in restricted account 
  at a Tier-1 Dominican bank (e.g., Banreservas)
- Purpose: Statutory guarantee only — not operational funds
- Quarterly rebalancing to inflation-adjusted thresholds
- Real-time reporting dashboard accessible by 
  Superintendencia de Bancos

---

## 2. Settlement Architecture: The USDC Bridge

- Asset: USDC (Circle) — fully reserved, US-regulated
- Network: Stellar
- SEP-31: Cross-border payment standardization
- SEP-10: Cryptographic authentication
- Liquidity: Maintained in Miami for large trade shipments

---

## 3. Fund Segregation Policy

- 100% of E-Float held in dedicated Custody Account
  at BCRD or authorized depository
- No commingling with corporate operating funds
- Bankruptcy remote — client funds not part of 
  corporate estate in insolvency
- Instant off-ramp: USDC → DOP via Pagos al Instante 
  (LBTR) upon supplier request

---

## 4. AML/CFT Compliance (Law 155-17)

- Automated OFAC, UN, and EU sanctions screening 
  on every transaction before broadcast to ledger
- SEP-12: Cryptographic KYC data exchange and storage
  for 10-year retention per Dominican law
- Compliance officer: DR resident, registered with UAF
